Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Mahakka

What makes Mahakka different from other social media platforms?

Mahakka combines social media with blockchain technology. Your content is permanently stored on the Bitcoin Cash blockchain, making it immutable and censorship-resistant. Additionally, our integrated token economy allows you to earn from your content while contributing to a deflationary token model.

How does the tipping system work?

When users tip your content, 80% goes directly to you as the creator, and 20% is allocated to the Mahakka development fund. This fund is used to buy back and burn Mahakka tokens, creating scarcity and potentially increasing the token's value over time.

What is the Mahakka token used for?

The Mahakka token is a CashToken on the Bitcoin Cash blockchain. It serves as the ecosystem token for our platform. As more people use Mahakka, more tokens are bought and burned, creating deflationary pressure. Users can acquire Mahakka tokens on Cauldron.Quest.

How often are tokens burned?

Tokens are burned on a weekly basis. The Mahakka development fund accumulates BCH from the 20% allocation of all tips throughout the week. Each week, this BCH is used to buy Mahakka tokens from the market, which are then sent to a burn address, permanently removing them from circulation.

Is my content really permanent?

Yes. When you post content through Mahakka, a transaction is created on the Bitcoin Cash blockchain containing a reference to your content (hosted on Imgur, Giphy, or YouTube) along with your hashtags and metadata. While the actual media files are hosted on these platforms, the reference and metadata are permanently stored on-chain, creating an immutable record of your post.

How does Mahakka benefit Bitcoin Cash?

Mahakka generates additional transaction volume for the Bitcoin Cash network through social media interactions. Each post, like, and tip creates blockchain transactions, generating miner fees and increasing network usage. This demonstrates practical use cases for blockchain technology beyond simple payments.
